Hello, world!

What is the origin of "Hello, world!"?

It's the phrase you see everywhere in coding. But where did it come from?

Have you every wondered where the phrase Hello, world! comes from? I have.

In fact, I just looked it up, and apparently, while its origin is somewhat unclear, it is commonly believed to have been first used by Brian Kernigham as example text in his book A Tutorial Introduction to the Language B.

Ever since, the phrase Hello, world! has been used to introduce people in the programming world to new things.
