THE PROGRESSIVE CAUSE

Who We Are

Not me. Us.

That is the essence of our message. We believe in looking after each other.

We believe that all of us have a right to life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness.

That means we should all have clear air to breathe, clean water to drink, food to eat, shelter from the elements, and affordable health care. We need those to live.

That means we should all have the liberty to learn through a good education. To speak freely, to love whom we wish, and to believe what we want, as long as we don't interfere with the liberties of our neighbors.

Then pursuit of happiness is up to us.

For all, not just the few.
