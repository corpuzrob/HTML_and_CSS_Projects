Who's side are you on? MJ or Scottie?


Scottie Pippen has expressed his anger and disappointment at the way Michael Jordan portrayed him in the series The Last Dance, saying that he (Jordan) couldn't have been more condescending.

Here are some reasons why each might be right:

Why Scottie Pippen is right:

  1. Spending so much time in The Last Dance focusing on Pippen choosing to sit out when Jackson called the final shot for Kukoc in game 3 against the Knicks is just spiteful: Jordan wasn't even playing then.
  2. Jordan consistently referred to the rest of his team as my supporting cast.
  3. Jordan never won a championship without Pippen.

Why Michael Jordan is right:

  1. MJ gave Scottie props in The Last Dance, saying Whenever they speak Michael Jordan, they should speak Scottie Pippen./li>
  2. Pippen choosing not to have his surgery earlier in 1998, along with getting a migraine in that game 7 against the Pistons, directly affected MJ and the Bulls.
  3. Pippen didn't do anything exceptional after MJ retired (the second time).
